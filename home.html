Dara Adeleye

I'm Dara Adeleye, a versatile artist from Lagos, Nigeria, now based in Calgary, Canada. From jazz bands in Annie Gale Middle School to delving into Computer Science and Animation at Nelson Mandela High School, my journey blends music and technology. I've applied my skills to enhance worship experiences in churches like New Covenant Assembly and Winners Chapel International Calgary. With Adobe Animate and programming languages, I craft digital and musical creations. Welcome to my world, where pixels and melodies intertwine.
