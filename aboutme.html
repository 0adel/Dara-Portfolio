About Me

Music

Thanks to having a music producer as a father, I've lived with musical instruments all around my house. We even have a full on music studio in our basement! Through using a software called Studio One, I've had the chance to develop lots of different songs throughout my life. If possible, I'd like to become the main music producer of a AAA game. Until then I'll stick to making songs for various other personal projects.

Animation

When I was really young I would make flip-books out of sticky notes. I'd always make them based off of some retro game (Street Fighter 2, PAC-MAN, Super Mario Bros, etc). Thanks to the influence of lots of cartoons, I decided to actually pursue it deeper. I first started off with Krita, but it didn't work out very well (It could also only do Raster graphics). After switching to Adobe Animate (with Vector graphics), I haven't looked back!
