My Education

Middle School Highschool Post-Secondary

Annie Gale Middle School

This school was really the start of my musical career. My father is a music producer, so I've been around music for most of my life. But it wasn't until I joined the Annie Gale Jazz Band that I really had the chance to explore music and play with a group of people. The then band instructor, Mr. O, has been a wonderful influence on my life. I played Saxophone for the first year and later became their pianist. I've got great memories here!

Nelson Mandela High School

This was the first time I had a school actually within walking distance of my house! My knowledge of music expanded as I joined the concert band of Nelson Mandela. I would also finally get deeper into animation and programming through joining dedicated classes to each. Unfortunately, all three of my years here would be in the middle of COVID, so playing was rather difficult. But it didn't deter me!

Mount Royal University

Right now I'm in my first year and second semester of my Bachelor of Computer Information Systems degree! It's been tough, but I think I'm getting along quite well. Naturally, the classes I've dealt with include Introduction to Programming, Web Design, and Marketing, although I hope to soon take some music courses once it becomes time for choosing electives!
